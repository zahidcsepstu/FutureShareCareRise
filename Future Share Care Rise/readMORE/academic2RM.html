Academic courses

During our academic life in BSc in Engg(CSE), we have to study various courses,but many times we don't know why have to learn programming courses,algorithms,database etc. Sometimes we try to memorise programming codes to achieve a minimum CGPA,that ultimately brings no result & our academic life becomes more difficult to survive.

Here we will discuss about some academic course's articles that should be practised and studied by the CSE undergraduate students around the whole world.in order to build a successful future with the best uses of academic courses we have to practise and work on various projects,write articles, make creative ideas based on our every semester study. For this task our course teachers will help us using this website.Here,teachers can upload articles, project metarials for their students based on their semester courses and by that students will gather knowledge about the future need,implementation of their academic courses.They will be interested in problem solving,system analysis,designing a project,writting article etc. Now we can visit some links that are given below for practising articles on some top courses.
